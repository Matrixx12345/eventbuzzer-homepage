Switzerland is full of unique attractions that go far beyond chocolate and watches. From spectacular natural wonders to historic sites to modern architectural highlights – here are the places you simply must see.

## Natural Wonders

### Rhine Falls near Schaffhausen
Europe's largest waterfall impresses with its tremendous power. You can take a boat right up to the thundering rocks – an unforgettable experience.

### Aletsch Glacier
The largest glacier in the Alps stretches over 23 kilometers. From the Eggishorn, you'll get a breathtaking panoramic view of this UNESCO World Heritage Site.

### Creux du Van
The Swiss Grand Canyon in the Neuchâtel Jura. A 160-meter-high rock amphitheater formed by erosion over millennia.

## Historic Sites

### Château de Chillon, Montreux
Switzerland's most visited historic building sits picturesquely on Lake Geneva. This medieval water castle inspired Lord Byron to write his famous poem.

### Chapel Bridge, Lucerne
Europe's oldest wooden bridge (1333) with its triangular paintings under the gable. The landmark of Lucerne and the most photographed spot in Switzerland.

### Old Town of Bern
The entire Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The 6-kilometer-long arcades form one of the longest covered shopping promenades in Europe.

## Modern Highlights

### Titlis Cliff Walk
Europe's highest suspension bridge at 3,041 meters. An adrenaline kick with Alpine panorama that is second to none.

### Staubbach Waterfall, Lauterbrunnen
The 297-meter free-falling waterfall in the Lauterbrunnen Valley. Goethe dedicated a poem to it – that's how impressive the sight is.

## Hidden Gems

- **Via Mala Gorge**: Dramatic gorge near Thusis with 300-meter-high rock walls
- **Lake Cauma, Flims**: Switzerland's most beautiful mountain lake with turquoise water
- **Gandria on Lake Lugano**: Switzerland's most romantic village, accessible only by boat
